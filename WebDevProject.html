Season Summary

Season 1 -

Mike is employed by Harvey, despite not having attended law school, and becomes his associate whilst learning the ways of the corporate law world.

Season 2 -

Harvey and Louis become senior partner. Daniel Hardman is then voted out of the firm as a result of his embezzlement. Edward darby proposes a merger to Jessica.

Season 3 -

Mike Learns that Rachel has applied to Stanford University, meaning she cannot join the firm. Pearson Darby Specter is Dissolved. Louis Has a heart attack in court.

Season 4 -

Mike leaves teh firm to become an investment banker and immediately comes up against Harvey. Louis finds out about Mike's secret and uses it as leverage to become a name partner.
