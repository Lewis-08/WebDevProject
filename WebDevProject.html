Season Summary

Season 1 -

Mike is employed by Harvey, despite not having attended law school, and becomes his associate whilst learning the ways of the corporate law world.

Season 2 -

Harvey and Louis become senior partner. Daniel Hardman is then voted out of the firm as a result of his embezzlement. Edward darby proposes a merger to Jessica.

Season 3 -

Mike Learns that Rachel has applied to Stanford University, meaning she cannot join the firm. Pearson Darby Specter is Dissolved. Louis Has a heart attack in court.

Season 4 -

Mike leaves the firm to become an investment banker and immediately comes up against Harvey. Louis finds out about Mike's secret and uses it as leverage to become a name partner. Mike re-joins the firm.

Season 5 -

Donna leaves Harvey to work for Louis. Mike proposes to Rachel. Mike is arrested on suspicion of fraud, with his case going to trial. Unable to deal with not knowing, He takes a plea deal that lands him two years behind bars.

Season 6 -

In light of Mike's arrest, everyone other than the name partners, Donna, Gretchen and Benjamin leave the firm, leaving them scrambling to stay afloat. Mike cuts a deal to get out of prison early by informing on his cellmate. Jessica leaves the firm.

Season 7 -

Harvey becomes managing partner. Alex Williams joins the firm as a senior partner. Donna kisses Harvey, even though he is in a realtionship. Harvey leaves the firm when they are under attack to go and help Jessica with a case surrounding her disbarrment in Chicago.

Season 8 -

Robert Zane joins the firm as a name partner and is voted the new managing partner, bringing Samantha Wheeler with him as a senior partner. Harvey must remind Samantha of teh pecking order when she questions him. Alex and Samantha come to blows over who should be the next name partner.
