Season Summary

Season 1 -

Mike is employed by Harvey, despite not having attended law school, and becomes his associate whilst learning the ways of the corporate law world. Mike is introduced to Rachel, who he immediately takes a liking to, even though she sees him as any other cocky lawyer.

Season 2 -

Harvey and Louis become senior partner. Daniel Hardman is then voted out of the firm as a result of his embezzlement. Edward darby proposes a merger to Jessica, meaning the firm must welcome on a host of new staff, including Stephen Huntley as a senior partner who proves to be more trouble than expected.

Season 3 -

Mike Learns that Rachel has applied to Stanford University, meaning she cannot join the firm. Pearson Darby Specter is Dissolved. Louis Has a heart attack in court.

Season 4 -

Mike leaves the firm to become an investment banker and immediately comes up against Harvey. Louis finds out about Mike's secret and uses it as leverage to become a name partner. Mike struggles to adapt to life as an investment banker and so re-joins the firm.

Season 5 -

Donna leaves Harvey to work for Louis. Mike proposes to Rachel. Mike is arrested on suspicion of fraud, with his case going to trial. Unable to deal with the uncertainty of a jury, he takes a plea deal that lands him two years behind bars.

Season 6 -

In light of Mike's arrest, everyone other than the name partners, Donna, Gretchen and Benjamin leave the firm, leaving them scrambling to stay afloat. Mike cuts a deal to get out of prison early by informing on his cellmate. Jessica leaves the firm to practice law in Chicago with her partner, Jeff Malone.

Season 7 -

Harvey becomes managing partner. Alex Williams joins the firm as a senior partner. Donna kisses Harvey, even though he is in a realtionship. Harvey temporarily leaves the firm when they are under attack to go and help Jessica with a case surrounding her disbarrment in Chicago. Mike and Rachel leave the firm entirely to run a new startup firm in Seattle.

Season 8 -

Robert Zane joins the firm as a name partner and is voted the new managing partner, bringing Samantha Wheeler with him as a senior partner. Harvey must remind Samantha of the pecking order when she questions him. Alex and Samantha come to blows over who should be the next name partner.

Season 9 -

Harvey and Donna become much closer. Mike returns to pick a fight with Harvey. The firm comes close to falling apart but the team manage to salvage enough to keep it together. Harvey and Donna get married, with Harvey deciding to leave the firm. Katrina becomes name partner along with Samantha and Alex.
